RoadMap

Phase 1: Project Launch

We will be birthing 3,333 Pocket Pigeons via the Solana Network; upon the mint of the pocket pigeons, holders will be given access to utilities. By working with our team of marketers and contracted promoters that our community knows and loves. Through mainstream marketing, we will be increasing the market demand for our beloved pocket pigeons. This alone will benefit the community by raising the price of their pigeon if they want to consider resale. However, holders will be rewarded with the profits reinvested in phase two.

Phase 2: Democracy of Reinvestment

As all of our friendly pocket pigeons get an owner, our team will reach out to our newfound community with plans of investing the profits of our pocket pigeons into different investments. We will practice a democratic system by allowing all members of the community to participate in polls related to where we should invest the pooled treasury.

Phase 3: Return of Investment

A majority of the profits from phase two will be distributed to current owners of the pigeons on a percentage basis of the individual pigeon value. Depending on the pigeon's rarity, holders will be airdropped a greater percent of the newfound profits.

Phase 4: Gifts Galore

Our team envisions phase four as a true celebration of our community and the long-awaited announcement of our project. We will reward our community with a giveaway that has no limits in scale. We're talking about something that has never been done before, and although it's risky, we believe it's worth it.

What Makes Us Unique?

What makes Pocket Pigeons unique compared to the rest of the crypto space? The Pocket Pigeons project is initially the first community-funded pool dedicated with the purpose of being reinvested and redistributed. Every holder can stake their Pocket Pigeon and reap the rewards from their holdings. Each Pocket Pigeon that is minted is guaranteed a set, direct percent of the Pocket Pigeons investment profits. Every holder is entitled to a say in which projects we as a team will reinvest our funds into. Pocket Pigeons offer each and every one of our holders a vote in which project we invest in through our democratic voting system. All of these special aspects set us apart from the competition. We are an organized and highly skilled group of individuals with a like-minded goal, creating the first and foremost best NFT investment project to date.
